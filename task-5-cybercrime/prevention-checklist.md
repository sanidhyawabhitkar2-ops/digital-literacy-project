Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

1) Never click links in emails that create urgency or panic — go directly to the official website instead.

2) Always check the sender's email address carefully — official institutions never use Gmail or Yahoo IDs.

3) Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts — email, GitHub, LinkedIn.

4) Never share your OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or university.

5) 💳 UPI Safety: Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger — scammers use "receive money" QR codes that actually deduct money.

6) 💳 UPI Safety: Set a daily UPI transaction limit in your banking app to minimize fraud risk.

7) Use a strong, unique password for each account — use a password manager if needed.

8) Report suspicious emails to your institution's IT helpdesk immediately and do not forward them.

9) If you are a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline 1930 (24x7).

10) Regularly review which apps have access to your Google/social media accounts and revoke unnecessary permissions.
